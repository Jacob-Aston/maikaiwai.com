Nā Mea Kanu

Nā mea kanu maoli nā kumu ola o ka ʻāina— e like me ka ʻōhiʻa lehua e kū pono i ka pele,
Native plants are the living roots of the land— like the ʻōhiʻa lehua standing strong against lava,
ke kalo e hānai ana i ka lāhui, a me ke ki e pale ana i ka ʻohana.

I kēia mau lā, hoʻopilikia nā mea kanu ʻē i ke kaiaola,

but by choosing Oʻahu's native plants and restoring balance,
hiki ke ulu hou ka nani a me ka ikaika o ka ʻāina me ka hana liʻiliʻi.
the land's beauty and resilience can flourish with minimal effort.

At Maikaʻi Wai,
kōkua mākou iā ʻoe e hoʻoulu i kēia kaiaola paʻa
we help you grow this enduring ecosystem
me ka ʻoluʻolu piha.
with complete satisfaction.

Holowai

ʻO ka wai ka waiwai nui loa— ke kahe ola e hānai ana i nā mea kanu a me ka ʻāina holoʻokoʻa.
Water is the greatest wealth— the flowing life that nourishes plants and the entire land.
Ma Oʻahu, he mea nui ka mālama pono ʻana i ka wai no ka pale ʻana i ka maloʻo a me ka hoʻopau nui.
On Oʻahu, proper water stewardship is essential to prevent drought and waste.
Ma Maikaʻi Wai, hoʻolālā mākou i nā ʻōnaehana holowai akamai i kūpono i ke anilā o kēia pae ʻāina,
At Maikaʻi Wai, we design smart irrigation systems suited to this island's climate,
e hōʻemi ana i nā bila a e hōʻoia i ka ulu maikaʻi mau me ka hana liʻiliʻi a me ka ʻoluʻolu piha.
reducing bills and ensuring consistent thriving growth with minimal effort and complete satisfaction.

Mālamalama (Illumination)

ʻO ka mālamalama ka hōʻike o ka mana pō— e hoʻāla ana i ka nani o ka ʻāina ma hope o ka napoʻo ʻana o ka lā, e like me nā hōkū i alakaʻi ai i nā kūpuna.
Illumination reveals the spirit of the night— awakening the land's beauty after sunset, like the stars that guided our ancestors.
Hiki ke lilo ka pō i wahi palekana ʻole inā ʻaʻole mālamalama pono, akā me nā ʻōnaehana maikaʻi e hoʻomālamalama i nā ala hele a me nā lāʻau niu,
Without proper light, the night can feel unsafe, but with well-designed systems highlighting pathways and palms,
e hoʻolilo ʻia ka ʻāina i wahi kupua a maluhia me ka mālama ikehu.
the land becomes a magical and peaceful sanctuary while conserving energy.
Ma Maikaʻi Wai,
At Maikaʻi Wai,
hana mākou i kēia mālamalama nani no ʻoe me ka maʻalahi.
we create this beautiful illumination for you effortlessly.
